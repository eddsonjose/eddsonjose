PROJECT 1

Eddson Jose

Carl Sagan's quote, "we are all made of star stuff" has been the inspiration for many of my works. My most recent work incorporates Sagan's quote in a manner of visual comparisons between nature and the artificial. Two entities (the forest and skyscrapers) seemingly opposite from one another are in actuality both made of the same materials according to Sagan's quote. The human mind distinguishes the differences between nature and the artificial despite their common origin. The reflection of the light is a representation of a star which also acts to embody the viewer themselves. My work incorporates the viewer as a light source or a star to depict the material that makes the entire universe and to depict the human mind that created the distinction between nature and the artificial.
