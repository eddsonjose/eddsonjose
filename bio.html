EDDSON JOSE | Artist Biography

Eddson Jose is a new media artist pursuing his BFA in San Jose State University. His work is mainly digital often using computer programs to create digital art that consist of digital representations of natural elements abstracted by a low polygon or low pixel count. One of his recent works is a multi-perspective outlook of a beneficial setting tracing the concept of the ownership of nature. His works exhibit an environmental attitude paired with a simplistic recreation of current environmental issues. Eddson has exhibited in the Herbert Sanders gallery and Gallery 5 in San Jose State University. He works and practices his art in San Jose, California.
