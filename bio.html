EDDSON JOSE | Artist Biography

Raised and works in San Jose California, Eddson Jose practices his skills in creating digital media art in his pursuit of a BFA in San Jose State University. Eddson grew up in the digital age but loved nature. He enjoys studying the relationship between nature and the manmade. Eddson also enjoys stargazing a result of searching for solidarity, peace, and quiet in the rapidly growing and bustling city of San Jose. His works exhibit an environmental attitude paired with a simplistic recreation of current environmental issues. His recent project involve a multi-perspective outlook of a beneficial setting tracing the concept of the ownership of nature. Other projects incorporate the idea of artificial creations in a digital galaxy.

Eddson has exhibited in the Herbert Sanders gallery, Gallery 5, and multiple display cabinets in San Jose State University.
