EDDSON JOSE | Artist Statement

Much of what I see today is digital. Like what I see my art follows. I enjoy landscapes, however, most of what I’ve seen I’ve never been to. I don’t recreate landscapes that I’ve seen but rather create landscapes from the experiences and emotions I lived through on the internet. Much of my inspirations come from a digital rendition of the physical world such as landscape photography shared online. I implement natural aspects of nature such as trees, mountains, animals, etc. and create the feeling of being there. For me, a successful landscape conveys the emotion or experience that I intend it to convey.
