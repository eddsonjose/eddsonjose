PORTFOLIO

What Keeps Us Afloat

Using the contrast of natural materials (wooden boats) and artificial ones (plastic sheets), the artwork showcases the relationship between the two materials and how one has overwhelmed and nearly contains the other. The projection is an overlay of a digital ocean that also serves as the background for the rivaling objects.

2019, Video Dimensions: 1920p x 1080p, Sculpture Dimensions: 14in x 7in x 10in, Medium: Projection and sculpture
