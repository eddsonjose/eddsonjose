Your Choice

As plastic pollution increases, humanity's efforts to fix the situation becomes more difficult. This artwork explores the user's stance on the situation and provides an opportunity for the user to virtually make a change. The user must make a series of decisions which will affect the user's final destination.

Controls: Mouse to look, WASD to move, Escape key to show the cursor.
