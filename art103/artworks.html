Wallpaper Renders


Wallpaper Renders is a personal project. It is a series of rendered images from a 3D environment I made in the Blender software. The context behind these images began in my transition into San Jose State University from Mission College in 2018. I was in search of a new laptop. I previously owned a Windows PC and was considering buying a Macbook mainly for its aesthetics as well as a considerable performance upgrade from my previous device. However, I didn't want to go through the process of learning a new operating system and the selection of Windows laptops had no equivalent option to the Macbook's design. In my frustration I wanted to design my own Windows laptop. This is also the moment that I learned how to use Blender. In creating my laptop I had used a wallpaper I downloaded online as a wallpaper for my laptop project. Before posting my laptop renders online I became aware of potential copyright issues. I then decided I wanted to create my own wallpaper specifically for my laptop project. Using the same program (Blender) I made a wallpaper. The result was above my expectations so I decided to create more resulting in the Wallpaper Renders. Overall, what started out as shopping for a laptop spiraled into the process of learning a new software, designing a laptop, and creating a whole series of wallpapers.

Book Project

Give and Take
Give and Take is a video project about the relationship between nature and mankind. The video showcases nature as an entity of "giving" and mankind as an entity of "taking."
